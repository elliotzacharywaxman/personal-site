Welcome

Hi, I'm Elliot. I'm the founder of an organization called ClimateDAO where we pool the wisdom and will of the crowds to get public companies to decarbonize (launching in Summer 22'). Before co-founding ClimateDAO, I was a researcher at KPMG's Innovation Lab where I focused on decarbonization, corporate governance, and blockchain.
